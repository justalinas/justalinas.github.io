Info: A Turned Based Simulation: Student VS. Homework

This is one of the Repl.it projects I made with my partner, Tristan Beuman.

The concept of the game is to outwit the "AI" (Studying in our case) is defeated.

The player must choose between a set of moves given in order to pass his studies

Both the hp levels go up to 100. For the player, indicating their stress level and studying/homework completed, for the AI.

If either you or the AI gets to 100, the other one wins.

We both want to credit a user by the name of acardnell24, who made the original formatting. But, we made edits / more definitions to make a lot more readable and so Tristan and I could understand it.
